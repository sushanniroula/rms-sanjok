About the College

Damak Multiple Campus is a QAAAccredited community-based campus.
It lies in Damak, Jhapa, Nepal and is affiliated to Tribhuvan University. It
is completely supported and managed by the community. With affiliation
from HSEB it offers 10+2 program in Science, Management, Humanities and Education.
